# Resources

## Data

[NeuroSeq](http://neuroseq.janelia.org): a collaboration with Adam Hantman, Nelson Spruston and others at Janelia Research Campus to produce high-precision transcriptional profiles from nearly 200 different cell types in the mouse nervous system.

## Reagents

[Enhancer trap](http://enhancertrap.bio.brandeis.edu): a collection of enhancer trap mouse strains generated at Brandeis in collaboration with Carlos Lois using lentiviral and piggyBac transposon-based transgenesis. Some of these strains are still available as live animals, others are available as frozen sperm or embryos.
